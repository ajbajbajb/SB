About Us / Studio20

Students in NYU’s Studio 20 master’s program, focusing on digital innovation in journalism, created a data-rich multimedia story that maps and charts food insecurity in the South Bronx. Instructed by Blake Hunsicker, we used Mapbox and D3, among other tools and languages, to build a content-rich story addressing a complex issue.
